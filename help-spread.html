General project share

The project in one post

The Waiting Room Stories Project collects real owner stories about emergency and specialist vet care becoming unreachable because of cost, insurance limits, upfront payment, or lack of fast support. Care existed. Love existed. The money did not. Read more or share your story: https://waitingroom.kingchillithepug.com/

Story request

Ask people to share

If emergency or specialist vet care became unreachable because of cost, insurance limits, upfront payment, reimbursement delays, or lack of fast support, your story can help. You do not need perfect details. A few sentences is enough. Share your story: https://waitingroom.kingchillithepug.com/share.html

Patterns update

Point people to the patterns

The Waiting Room Stories Project is starting to show the same problems coming up again and again: upfront payment, insurance limits, delayed reimbursement, and care becoming unaffordable when time matters. See what people are sharing so far: https://waitingroom.kingchillithepug.com/patterns.html
