What needs to change

Make emergency care easier to understand and reach.

There is no single fix. The project is building evidence for practical changes that could reduce the gap between available care and reachable care.

Clearer pet insurance

Families should understand limits, exclusions, referral wording, diagnostic limits, and reimbursement timing before an emergency.

Better emergency warnings

Policies should make it easier to see how quickly specialist, ICU, imaging, hospitalisation, or surgery costs can exceed cover.

More direct payment options

Where possible, insurers and veterinary providers should make direct-to-vet payment easier and clearer.

Faster bridge options

Payment, grant, or authorisation routes need to work on emergency timelines. Slow processes do not solve decisions that happen within hours.

Better crisis information

Families need clear, kind explanations of likely costs, options, limits, and next steps during the decision window.

Careful public evidence

Public summaries should show patterns without exposing people, blaming veterinary teams, or overpromising policy change.
