What we want to change

Make emergency access easier to understand and reach.

The project is not promising a simple fix. It is building evidence for practical changes that could reduce the gap between available care and reachable care.

These are change goals, not services currently offered by the project.

Clearer pet insurance

Owners should understand limits, exclusions, referral wording, diagnostic limits, and reimbursement timing before an emergency.

Emergency limit warnings

Policies should make it easier to see how quickly specialist, ICU, imaging, or surgery costs can exceed cover.

More direct payment options

Where possible, insurers and veterinary providers should make direct-to-vet payment easier and clearer.

Faster bridge options

Payment, grant, or authorisation routes need to work on emergency timelines. Slow processes do not solve decisions that happen within hours.

Better crisis information

Families need clear, kind explanations of likely costs, options, limits, and next steps during the decision window.

Evidence before asks

The project will use pilot patterns and anonymised consented stories to shape realistic asks, not overclaim policy impact.
