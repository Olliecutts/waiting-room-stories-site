FOR PET OWNERS

Share what happened when care became hard to reach.

This form is for pet owners who had to stop, delay, limit, or question emergency or specialist veterinary care because of cost, insurance limits, upfront payment, reimbursement delays, or lack of fast support.

Share your story

What to write

A few sentences is enough.

You do not need perfect details, medical wording, exact dates, or exact bills. Explain what care was being discussed, what got in the way, and what would have helped.

Who should share

Pet owners who faced an emergency or specialist-care decision where money, insurance, payment timing, or support affected what felt reachable.

What happens next

Your story helps show patterns. Public pages use charts and summaries, not raw private stories.

If your pet needs help now

This project cannot provide urgent help, money, grants, or professional advice. Speak directly with your vet or emergency hospital.

Separate route

Work around these decisions?

Professional Insight has its own separate form and does not add to the owner story count. Share professional insight.
