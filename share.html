This form is for pet owners who had to stop, delay, limit, or question emergency or specialist veterinary care because of cost, insurance limits, upfront payment, or lack of fast support.

Open the story form

Who it is for

People in the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, or elsewhere who faced a cost or payment barrier around emergency or specialist veterinary care.

What to share

A few sentences is enough. You do not need exact dates, medical wording, invoices, policy documents, photos, or perfect detail.

Anonymous use

The form asks whether anonymous quotes may be used later. Nothing identifying should be published without permission and human review.

What this is not

This is not an emergency support service, charity, fund, or advice service. It cannot help with current vet bills.

If your pet needs help now

Please speak directly with your vet or emergency hospital. The story form is for evidence collection, not urgent help or advice.
