Share your emergency vet care story

You can write as little as you want.

This form is for pet owners who had to stop, delay, limit, or question emergency or specialist veterinary care because of cost, insurance limits, upfront payment, or lack of fast financial support.

Open the story form

Who it is for

People in the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, or elsewhere who faced a cost or payment barrier around emergency or specialist veterinary care.

What to share

A few sentences is enough. You do not need exact dates, medical wording, invoices, policy documents, or perfect detail.

What this is not

This is not an emergency support service, charity, fund, or advice service. It cannot help with current vet bills.

Anonymous use

The form asks whether anonymous quotes may be used later. Nothing should be published without consent and manual review.

Before you start

If your pet is currently in emergency care, speak directly with your vet or emergency hospital. The project form is for evidence collection, not urgent help.
