Share your story

A few sentences can help.

This form is for pet owners who had to stop, delay, limit, or question emergency or specialist veterinary care because of cost, insurance limits, upfront payment, reimbursement delays, or lack of fast support.

You do not need perfect details. You do not need medical wording. You do not need exact dates or bills. If all you can manage is a few sentences, that is enough.

Open the story form

Who it is for

Pet owners in the UK, USA, Canada, Australia, or elsewhere who faced a cost, insurance, or payment barrier around emergency or specialist veterinary care.

What to share

What happened, what care was needed, what got in the way, and what might have helped.

Anonymous use

The form asks whether your story can be used anonymously later. Anything public will be reviewed carefully first.

If your pet needs help now

This project cannot provide urgent help or professional advice. If your pet is in emergency care now, speak directly with your vet or emergency hospital.

Help more families find it.

On mobile, the share button should let you choose apps like WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Messages, and more if they are available on your device.
