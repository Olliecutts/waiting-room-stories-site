For professionals

We need professional insight too

Owner stories are only one part of the picture. If you are a vet, vet nurse, receptionist, referral clinician, practice manager, charity worker, insurer, payment provider, researcher, or work anywhere in animal healthcare, we would value your perspective.

This is not an anti-vet project. Veterinary teams are often the people standing beside families during impossible decisions. We want to understand the funding, insurance, and payment gaps around those moments, not blame the people trying to help.

What would help

Insight that makes the evidence more useful and balanced.

We are especially interested in the pressure points professionals see around urgent decisions, cost conversations, insurance cover, payment timing, and referral access.

Suggested subject: Professional insight: Waiting Room Stories

What insight helps

How emergency payment decisions work, where insurance claims slow down, where direct payment helps, and where families lose time.

What we are not asking for

Please do not send client records, invoices, policy documents, case notes, or identifiable patient details.

What the project needs

Clear, practical input on what would make emergency and specialist care easier to reach without blaming owners or veterinary teams.

Useful topics

Upfront payment, reimbursement delays, referral transfers, ICU and imaging costs, direct-to-vet payment, hardship processes, charity limits, bridge funding, policy clarity, and crisis communication.
