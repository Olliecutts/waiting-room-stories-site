A separate evidence track is open.

Owner stories show what families experienced. Professional insight helps explain what is happening around those moments.

Share professional insight

Why this track exists

Owner stories and professional insight answer different questions.

Owner stories show what happened to families at the decision point. Professional Insight can help explain the systems, constraints, processes, and practical changes around those moments.

Who should respond

Veterinary, insurance, payment, charity, research, animal welfare, and access-related professionals.

What the form collects

Multiple-choice answers feed separate anonymous Professional Insight charts. Written answers are reviewed before any short anonymous excerpt is used.

How it stays separate

Professional Insight does not add to the owner story total. The public page shows reviewed aggregate patterns only.

Professional Insight charts

Reviewed anonymous Professional Insight multiple-choice responses only. These charts stay separate from owner story charts.

Professional insight charts will appear here once the first responses have been reviewed.

Non-blame framing

This is about access, not attacking veterinary teams.

Veterinary teams are often standing with families during the hardest decisions. This project is focused on payment timing, insurance limits, support routes, communication, and care access.
