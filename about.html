About the project

Born from Chilli's story, built for something wider.

Chilli's illness is the reason this project started. After we asked for support with his care, we saw story after story from people who had faced the same impossible moment: care was available, but the money or payment system put it out of reach.

The Waiting Room Stories Project is our attempt to use the platform built around Chilli to collect those experiences carefully and turn them into evidence for change.

It is about emergency and specialist veterinary care broadly: ICU, imaging, surgery, referral, cancer and complex care, emergency stabilisation, ongoing treatment, and the payment barriers around them.

The heart of it

Care existed. Love existed. The money did not.

Families should not be left alone at the exact moment when treatment is possible but access depends on immediate money, insurance wording, or reimbursement timing.

What it collects

Short owner accounts about cost, insurance limits, upfront payment, reimbursement delays, and lack of fast support.

What it protects

Names, contact details, raw stories, and identifying details are not part of public pattern summaries.

What it is not

It is not veterinary advice, legal advice, financial advice, insurance advice, emergency support, or a fundraising service.

What it hopes to change

Clearer insurance, better payment routes, faster bridge options, and better information for families facing impossible decisions.
