About

Born from Chilli's story, built for broader evidence.

Chilli's case is the origin story, but the project is about emergency and specialist veterinary care more broadly: ICU, imaging, surgery, referral, cancer and complex care, emergency stabilisation, and the payment barriers around them.

The heart of it

Care existed. Love existed. The money did not.

Families can be left alone at the exact moment when care exists but money decides whether that care can be reached. The Waiting Room Stories Project exists to show those patterns clearly and carefully.

What it collects

Short owner accounts about cost, insurance limits, upfront payment, reimbursement delays, and lack of fast support.

What it protects

Names, contact details, raw stories, and identifying details are not part of public pattern summaries.

What it is not

It is not a replacement for veterinary advice, legal advice, financial advice, insurance advice, or emergency support.

What it hopes to change

Insurance clarity, direct payment options, emergency bridge support, and better information for families facing impossible choices.
