FAQ

Short answers.

For privacy, anonymous use, removal requests, and story-use detail, read How stories are used.

Is this blaming vets?

No. Veterinary teams are often the people standing with families during the hardest decisions. The project is about access, payment timing, insurance limits, and support gaps.

Can I share anonymously?

Yes. The form asks about anonymous use. Public pages do not publish names or private stories. More detail is on How stories are used.

What happens to my story?

Your story helps show the bigger pattern. Public updates use charts and summaries, not raw private stories.

Can you help with current vet bills?

No. The project cannot provide urgent help, money, grants, or professional advice. If your pet needs help now, speak directly with your vet or emergency hospital.

Are you a charity or fund?

No. This is a public evidence project collecting stories and showing patterns around emergency and specialist vet care access.

Can professionals contact you?

Yes. Professional insight is welcome, especially from people who work around emergency care, insurance, payment, charity, research, or animal welfare. Use the Professional insight page.
