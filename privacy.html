Privacy / how stories are used

Stories are handled carefully.

The project uses stories to understand patterns. It should not publish names, contact details, raw full stories, or identifying details.

What may happen after submission

A story may be read, tagged for patterns, and included in aggregate counts. Anonymous quotes are considered only where permission allows and after manual review.

What should not be public

Pet names, owner names, email addresses, exact locations, vet names, insurer names, claim numbers, and raw full stories should not appear on public pages.

Contact route

For pilot admin/contact/withdrawal requests only: waitingroom@kingchillithepug.com. This is not an emergency support or advice route.

Before public launch

Privacy wording and retention rules should be legally reviewed before any wider public launch or formal report release.
