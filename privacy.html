How stories are used

We do not publish names or private stories.

This page explains privacy, anonymous use, quote permission, the urgent-help boundary, and common questions.

What public pages use.

Public pages use charts, summaries, and careful project updates. Written answers are reviewed before any short anonymous excerpt is used.

Owner stories

Owner stories feed owner-story patterns only.

Professional Insight

Professional Insight is a separate evidence track and does not add to the owner story count.

Not urgent help

This project cannot provide emergency help, money, grants, legal advice, veterinary advice, financial advice, or insurance advice.

FAQ.

Do you publish names or private stories?

No. Public pages do not publish names or private stories.

Can I share anonymously?

Yes. The form asks about anonymous use, and public updates use charts and summaries.

Can you help with current vet bills?

No. The project cannot provide urgent help, money, grants, or professional advice. If your pet needs help now, speak directly with your vet or emergency hospital.

Is this blaming vets?

No. Veterinary teams are often the people standing with families during the hardest decisions. The project is about access, payment timing, insurance limits, and support gaps.

Can professionals respond?

Yes. Professional Insight has a separate route and does not add to the owner story count.
