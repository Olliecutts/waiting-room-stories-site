How stories are used

How stories are used

Carefully, anonymously, and with the purpose of showing patterns.

Stories shared with the project are used to understand repeated barriers in emergency and specialist veterinary care.

We look for patterns such as what care was needed, what stopped access, whether insurance helped or failed, and what could have made a difference.

What public pages avoid

Public pages should not include names, contact details, pet names, exact locations, vet names, insurer names, claim details, or raw full stories.

How patterns are found

Stories may be grouped by care needed, access barriers, insurance position, outcomes, and what would have helped.

Anonymous quotes

Anonymous quotes may only be considered where permission allows and after human review.

Contact route

For pilot admin, contact, or withdrawal requests: waitingroom@kingchillithepug.com.

If your pet is in emergency care now

Please speak directly with your vet or emergency hospital. This project cannot provide urgent support or professional advice.
